Our Studio

Precision in every stitch.

Northbest International is a premium apparel manufacturing company based in Guwahati, offering end-to-end production for streetwear and custom clothing. We specialize in low-MOQ, high-quality garments for brands, businesses, and creators across India.

We started in 2017 with a challenge – finding a manufacturer who could deliver premium streetwear without demanding excessive volumes. Instead of waiting for the right partner, we built the facility ourselves.
